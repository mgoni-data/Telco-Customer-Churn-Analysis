# Telco-Customer-Churn-Analysis
## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn for a telecommunication company using Excel, SQL and Tableau. The key objectives id to identify the key factors associated with customer churn, quantify the business impact, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Business problem
The company is experiencing customer attrition and wants to understand:
  > Which customers are most likely to churn?
  > What factors are associated with churn?
  > Which customer segments should be prioritized for retention?
  > How churn affects business revenue?

## Tools Used
  > Excel: Initial data exploration, and cleaning
  > MySQL: Data analysis
  > Tableau Public: Interactive dashboard and visualization.


## Key Analyses
  > Overall churn rate
  > Churn by contract type
  > Tenure analysis
  > Monthly charges analysis
  > Tech support and internet service analysis
  > High-risk customer segmentation
  > Revenue at risk estimation


## Key Findings
  > Approximately 26.6% customers have churn
  > Customers on mpnth-to-month contract type have the highest churn rate
  > Customers with short tenure are most likely to leave.
  > Customers paying higher monthly charges tend to churn more frequently.
  > Certain customer segments,particularly thise with month-to-month contracts and fiber optic service, exhibit elevated churn risk.


## Business Recommendations
  > Strenghten customer onboarding during the first few months.
  > Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
  > Encourage migration from month-to-month to longer-term contracts through incentives.
  > Investigate the relationship between pricing, service quality and customer churn.
  > Monitor high-risk customer segments proactively to reduce revenue loss.
