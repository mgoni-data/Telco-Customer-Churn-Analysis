# Key Insights


## Customer Churn
  > Customers on Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate (42.71%).
  > Two-year contracts had the lowest churn rate

## Internet Service/Tech support
  > Fiber optic customers experience the highest churn.
  > Customers without tech support churned more frequently.
 

## Recommendations
  > Target Month-to-month customers with retention offers.
  > Encourage migration to longer-term contracts.
  > Increase Tech support adoption among high-risk customers.
